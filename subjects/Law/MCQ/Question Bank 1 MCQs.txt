Q: What is the primary purpose of Human Rights law?
A) To protect state interests
B) To protect individual dignity and freedom
C) To increase government power
D) To regulate international trade
Answer: B
Explanation: Human rights are inherent to all human beings, regardless of race, sex, nationality, ethnicity, language, religion, or any other status.

Q: The Universal Declaration of Human Rights was adopted in:
A) 1918
B) 1945
C) 1948
D) 1960
Answer: C
Explanation: The UDHR was adopted by the UN General Assembly on 10 December 1948.
